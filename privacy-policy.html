Privacy Policy

Smart Job respects user privacy and protects account information, applications, resumes, and profile data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details such as name, email, role, and password.
  • Profile details such as phone number, bio, location, website, and profile photo.
  • Job posts, applications, uploaded resumes, and saved jobs.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used to provide login, job posting, job application, application tracking, recruiter contact, and profile features.

3. Data Protection

Passwords are stored in hashed form. The website uses authentication checks so users can only access allowed routes. The assistant does not reveal confidential information such as passwords, tokens, private resumes, or another user’s hidden data.

4. Uploaded Files

Resumes and profile images are used only for job application and profile features. Users should not upload harmful or illegal files.

5. User Control

Users can update their profile information from the profile page. Recruiters can manage their job posts, and candidates can view their applications.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact page.
